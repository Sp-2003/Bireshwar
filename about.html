About Us

Bireshwar Cold Storage is one of the trusted names in agricultural storage solutions, serving the farming community of West Bengal since 1995.

We specialize in the cold storage of potatoes and other perishable goods, ensuring their freshness and longevity. With a modern facility, efficient management, and a commitment to quality, we help farmers and traders reduce post-harvest loss and increase profitability.

Our Mission

To provide reliable, secure, and cost-effective storage solutions for agricultural produce, supporting rural prosperity and food sustainability.

Our Vision

Becoming a leading agro-storage service in Eastern India with an emphasis on technology, transparency, and trust.
